About IdeaStruct

IdeaStruct is an AI agent designed to help people turn raw ideas into simple, structured Lean Business Plans.

Many people have ideas but struggle with fear or decision fatigue. IdeaStruct removes that friction by listening first and then organizing thoughts into a clear, consistent framework.

The agent is intentionally focused. It does not provide advice, answer general questions, or guess facts. Its job is to create clarity at the earliest stage of an idea.
