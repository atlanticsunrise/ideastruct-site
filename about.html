About IdeaStruct

IdeaStruct was created for the moment when an idea first appears. That moment is often messy, unstructured, and easy to lose. Many people don’t struggle with creativity, they struggle with knowing where to begin.

The problem isn’t laziness. It’s fear and decision fatigue. When everything feels possible, taking the first step can feel overwhelming. IdeaStruct reduces that friction by turning raw thoughts into a clear, structured Lean Business Plan.

IdeaStruct is not a chatbot. It doesn’t ask endless questions or push users toward execution. It listens first, then organizes information into a consistent framework that makes ideas easier to evaluate and move forward.

Built with IBM watsonx Orchestrate, IdeaStruct demonstrates how focused, agentic AI can deliver real value by knowing when to act, how to structure information, and when to stop.
