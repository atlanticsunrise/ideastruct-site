Why IdeaStruct

The problem isn’t laziness. It’s fear and decision fatigue. When everything feels possible, taking the first step can feel overwhelming. IdeaStruct removes that friction by giving your ideas a simple, plausible structure you can actually evaluate.

Not a chatbot

IdeaStruct doesn’t ask endless questions or send you down a rabbit hole. It doesn’t guess facts or offer advice. It takes what you say and organizes it into a consistent business framework, so you can decide what’s worth exploring next.
